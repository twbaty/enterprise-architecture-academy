# Guiding Principles

## Teach the problem before the framework
Begin with a recognizable enterprise problem.

## Architecture is decision support
Every concept must connect to a decision, concern, trade-off, governance need, or change outcome.

## Foundation and Practitioner remain distinct
Foundation emphasizes understanding. Practitioner emphasizes application and analysis.

## Minimum useful architecture
More artifacts are not inherently better.

## Reality outranks the model
Baseline architecture must be evidence-based.

## Governance must enable delivery
Governance means decision rights, accountability, exceptions, compliance, and feedback—not ceremony.
