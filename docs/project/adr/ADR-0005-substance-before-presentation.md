# ADR-0005: Substance precedes presentation

**Status:** Accepted

## Context

Early effort can be consumed by logos, landing pages, badges, and visual design while curriculum structure and content remain incomplete.

## Decision

The project prioritizes governance, source authority, traceability, curriculum architecture, migration, and instructional content before nonessential visual refinement.

## Consequences

- The site may remain visually simple during early releases.
- Roadmap work is evaluated by learning value and maintainability first.
- Presentation improvements are accepted when they solve a real usability problem.
